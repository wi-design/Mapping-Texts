Assessing Language Patterns: A Look at Texas Newspapers, 1829-2008

This visualization plots the language patterns embedded in 232,567 pages of historical Texas newspapers, as they evolved over time and space. For any date range and location, you can browse the most common words (word counts), named entities (people, places, etc), and highly correlated words (topic models). [ read more ]
